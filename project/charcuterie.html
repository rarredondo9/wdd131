Charcuterie Board

Create a stunning charcuterie board with a mix of cheeses, meats, fruits, and crackers. Perfect for parties, family gatherings, or a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • Selection of cheeses (cheddar, brie, gouda, etc.)
  • Assorted cured meats (salami, prosciutto, etc.)
  • Fresh fruits (grapes, berries, figs)
  • Crackers and breadsticks
  • Olives and pickles
  • Small bowls of nuts and jellys

Instructions

  1. Choose a large wooden board or platter.
  2. Arrange cheeses evenly across the board.
  3. Fill in spaces with cured meats, folding them neatly.
  4. Add bowls of olives, nuts, and dips.
  5. Fill remaining gaps with fruits and crackers.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
