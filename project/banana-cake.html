Banana Cake

This banana cake is soft, moist, and bursting with banana flavor. It's a perfect treat for tea time, dessert, or even breakfast. Easy to bake and loved by all ages!

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cup of mashed bananas (ripe)
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, whisk the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt together. Set aside.
  3. In another bowl, beat the butter on high speed until smooth and creamy.
  4. Add both sugars and beat on high speed until creamed together.
  5. Add egg and vanilla and mashed bananas and beat until combined.
  6. Add the dry ingredients and milk alternating until incorporated.
  7. Put it in a the cake pan and bake for 45-50 min
  8. ENJOY!
