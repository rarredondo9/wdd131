Raspberry Cheesecake

Smooth and creamy cheesecake with a tangy raspberry topping. A dessert that's as beautiful as it is delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 3 packages (8 oz each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup raspberry preserves
  • Fresh raspberries for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease a springform pan.
  2. Mix crumbs, sugar, and melted butter; press into pan bottom.
  3. Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, then vanilla.
  4. Pour over crust and bake for 50-55 minutes. Cool completely.
  5. Top with raspberry preserves and garnish with fresh raspberries.
  6. Chill before serving.
