Site Name

Lava Kitchen

This page is themed around the Minecraft movie and gameplay aesthetics. My kids love Minecraft and this site will be their go-to for my themed recipes!

Site Purpose

The purpose of Lava Kitchen is to be a kid-friendly recipe website inspired by Minecraft. It includes fun, safe, easy-to-follow recipes.

Scenarios

  • Which recipe looks like more fun to do?
  • Can I print out a recipe to make with Mom?
  • Which recipes are my favorites?

Color Scheme

  • Pixel Green: Used for navigation bars and headings (#4CAF50)
  • Block Brown: Background and accents (#5D4037)

Typography

  • Press Start 2P (Google Font): Used for headings, gives the Minecraft pixel-style feel
  • Open Sans: Used for body content, easy for kids to read
